About

History

JCOM, which stands for Jaycees Chamber of Commerce, is a business program within JCI (Junior Chamber International) that focuses on networking and developing business opportunities for JCI members. JCOM also recognizes JCI members based on the size of their tables, with different levels of recognition awarded during events like NATCON. JCOM is active in Tamil Nadu, with events and local chapters like JCOM Zone 17 and JCOM L Tirupur.
