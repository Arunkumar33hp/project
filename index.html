Who we are

About Us

JCOM (Jaycees Chamber of Commerce) is a dynamic initiative under JCI India, dedicated to empowering young entrepreneurs and business leaders across Tamil Nadu.

JCOM chapters in Tamil Nadu are contributing to the growth of the entrepreneurial ecosystem by providing valuable resources, support, and opportunities for young entrepreneurs.

To nurture entrepreneurial spirit through impactful training, mentorship, and capacity-building programs.To facilitate meaningful networking and collaboration among entrepreneurs, industry leaders, and stakeholders. To promote ethical business practices and inclusive economic growth across all JCOM chapters..

Our History

JCOM, which stands for Jaycees Chamber of Commerce, is a business program within JCI (Junior Chamber International) that focuses on networking and developing business opportunities for JCI members. JCOM also recognizes JCI members based on the size of their tables, with different levels of recognition awarded during events like NATCON.

JCOM is active in Tamil Nadu, with events and local chapters like JCOM Zone 17 and JCOM L Tirupur.
